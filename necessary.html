Corona Virus - COVID-19

The necessary precautions :

To prevent the spread of Covid-19 disease, do the following: 1-Keep cleaning your hands. Use soap and water or an alcohol solution to sterilize the hands. 2-Keep a safe distance from anyone coughing or sneezing. 3-Do not touch your eyes, nose, or mouth. 4-Make sure to cover your mouth and nose by bending the elbow or a tissue with coughing or sneezing. 5-Go home if you feel sick. 6-If you have a fever, cough, and trouble breathing, seek medical attention. And call the care facility before you go. 7-Follow the directions of the local health authorities. If Corona's disease has spread widely in any country, that country must declare a complete ban on that region and provide hospitals and equip them for the conditions that must be present, so the hospital.
